Featured Today

India's apex predator

The Bengal Tiger — India's national animal and most iconic wildlife species. From a low of fewer than 2,000 in 1973, Project Tiger has brought their numbers back to over 4,500. A story of what conservation can achieve.

⭐ Animal of the Day
Bengal Tiger
Panthera tigris tigris

India's apex predator and national animal. Each tiger's stripe pattern is as unique as a human fingerprint — the basis of India's entire camera-trap census for Project Tiger.
