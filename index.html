Bengal Tiger

India's most iconic wildlife

Featured Today

India's apex predator

The Bengal Tiger — India's national animal and most iconic species. From a critical low of fewer than 2,000 in 1973, Project Tiger has brought numbers back to over 4,500. A story of what conservation can achieve when a nation decides to act.
